Yoga

Derived from the Sankrit word ‘yuj’ which means ‘to unite or integrate’, yoga is a 5,000-year-old Indian body of knowledge. Yoga is all about harmonizing the body with the mind and breath through the means of various pranayamas, yoga asanas (yoga poses) and meditation.

Mediation

Today, meditating daily is not a luxury but a necessity. To be unconditionally happy and to have peace of mind, we need to tap into the power of meditation. The purpose of meditation is to make one calm, stress-free, free from pain, healthy and happy. Meditation helps in maintaining good physical health, mental health & emotional health.
